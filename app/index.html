She's Gonna Take A Picture Too

    As of last week, it's been over two years since we've released a new album and although we've been sharing bits and peices of new music to come, we haven't actually put anything "out." Naturally, that's been driving us crazy and we've decided to change that.

    On Tuesday October 22nd at 10AM CST on this same URL there will be a free stream and pay-what-you-want download of a new single called "She's Gonna Take A Picture Too." If you'd like to hear and download the song early, come to our show this Saturday at One Eyed Jacks. We will be performing it live as well as giving out download cards at our merch booth for the song as a special thanks.
